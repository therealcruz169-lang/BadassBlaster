BadassBlaster - Quick Play & Share

Files:
- BadassBlaster.html : Single-file game. Open in any modern browser.
- README.txt : This file.

How to use:
1. Unzip BadassBlaster.zip.
2. Double-click BadassBlaster.html (or right-click -> Open with -> your browser).
3. Play! Move your mouse (or finger on mobile) to move left/right.
   Click or tap to shoot. Keyboard: Arrow keys or A/D to move, Space to shoot.

Notes:
- Enemies spawn faster and move faster over time (difficulty ramps up).
- No internet required to play (except if you click the Cash App support link).
- To host online later, upload the HTML file to GitHub Pages, Netlify, or any static file host.
